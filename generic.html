My journey to the
technical writing

“As a technical communicator, I am an enabler of information.”

My path as a technical writer has started in 2014, when I had published my research article in an international journal with a high impact factor.


Later on, I fell in love with technical writing, and I used to spend hours learning about software, authoring and editing tools, conducting SME interviews, and drafting technical content that is useful for the target audiences, among other things.

At that time, I was not knowing the exciting challenges that yet to come!.

Nowadays, I keep seeking new skills, learning and sharing my humble knowledge as much as possible. My latest hobby now is learning HTML and building interesting websites.
