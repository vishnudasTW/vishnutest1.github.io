The beginner's guide to
technical writing


A guide for newbie technical writers to understand the fundamentals


Technical writing is one of the most demanding job now. 'The beginner’s guide to technical writing' aims to provide more insights into technical writing fundamentals and its career perspectives. This book is helpful for newbie technical writers and for those who wants to know basics of technical writing.

This book also covers information on the skills that required for a technical writer, benefits of technical writing career, how to become a technical writer and so on. Definity, ‘The beginner’s guide to technical writing’ is a must-read book for both newcomers and those who want to switch to technical writing career.

Vishnu Das, who is the writer of this book is an experienced documentation and information development specialist with multidomain experience including the scientific and software industries. The main ideas are explained by the author in a clear and concise manner.
